Therapies provided by compassionate and experienced specialists that will reinforce your path towards recovery

Cognitive and Dialectical Behavioral Therapies

These two therapy approaches are centered around allowing clients to explore the mental and emotional challenges which hinder the recovery process.

Experiential Therapy

This trauma-informed approach to group and individual therapy explores the traumatic events of one’s past which may contribute to ongoing substance abuse.

12 Steps and Self-Help Groups

12 Steps and other self-help groups are designed to help maintain a focus on the recovery process and self-improvement.

Family Counseling

Because addiction is a disease which impacts all areas of one’s life, repairing bonds and relationships among family members and loved ones is an important step in the recovery process.

Life Skills Training

All Niznik Behavioral Health facilities provide actionable life skills training such as basic cleanliness and hygiene, communication skills, and mindfulness.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Long Are The Programs?

Our detox programs  are designed to sever the physical dependency aspects of addiction in the fastest, safest, and most comfortable means possible. This process usually ranges between 7 to 10 days, but this may differ depending on the individual needs of each client. The duration of our treatment programs is generally between 30 and 90 days but may also vary.

When can I start?

Today. One of our Treatment Specialists will tend to your needs and verify your insurance/financial information, as well as guide you through the process of selecting the appropriate treatment center most suitable for you.
